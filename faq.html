Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with TechStore.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most products in their original condition. Please check our Returns page for detailed instructions.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days within the US. Expedited options are available at checkout.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to select international destinations. Shipping costs and times vary by location. Please see our Shipping page for details.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order using the order ID provided in your confirmation email. Visit our Track Order page to check the status.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are secure.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be canceled or modified within 24 hours of placement. Please contact our support team at support@techstore.com.
